Accrue's Big Day!

Everyone can now get the app on Android and iOS.

Accrue Startups Launch

Today's a big day for everyone on the Accrue team. We're officially coming out of private beta and launching to the general public!

We started Accrue to solve a problem we had with investing and it's been amazing to see it grow from our personal problem solver to having over a thousand users and a current AUM of ~ $150k within a few months (in private beta).

All of this time has been spent building and listening to users, and I'm incredibly proud of the effort of the entire team.

Our private beta was immensely successful, and we're thankful to all of our users, whose valuable feedback has brought us to this point.

When you're new to investing, it's extremely difficult to time the market / not give in to FOMO while figuring out the best time to buy an asset. Accrue allows you to invest in your favourite assets for as low as $1 using dollar-cost averaging (DCA).

With dollar-cost averaging, you mitigate the risk of price volatility & emotional investing by spreading your purchases across pre-defined intervals. This means that instead of constantly searching for (and missing) the dip with a lump sum purchase, your purchases will be spread out on a daily (or weekly/bi-weekly/monthly) basis. All of this done regardless of the changes in the price of the asset you choose to purchase.

Accrue allows you to do this with over 100 assets, and if you don't find your preferred asset listed, kindly tweet at us or send us an email, and we'll work on listing that asset ASAP.

Over the next few months, we're going to be working on several features to make Accrue your one-stop shop for all of your investment needs. We also have plans to open up to other countries in a few weeks.

We're in exciting times, and we can't wait for you to see all that we have planned.

Thank you so much for using Accrue!

Zino Asamaige

Co-Founder & COO at Accrue
