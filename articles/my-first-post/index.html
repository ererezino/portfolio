My First Post

personal thoughts
Zino Asamaige

Building things in Lagos

This is my first post on this blog. Just testing things out and seeing how it all works.

I've been meaning to start writing more. There's something about putting thoughts into words that helps clarify what you actually think. Plus, it's nice to have a record of where your head was at different points in time.

Why now?

I've had this website for a while, but it's mostly been a static page. A few photos, some links. The basics. But I wanted a place to share longer thoughts, not just for others, but for myself.

Writing is thinking. And I could use more of both.

"The first draft is just you telling yourself the story."

What to expect

I'm not sure yet what this will become. Probably a mix of:

  • Things I'm learning while building Accrue
  • Travel stories and photos
  • Random observations about life
  • Maybe some technical stuff

We'll see where it goes. The important thing is to start.

If you made it this far, thanks for reading. More to come.
