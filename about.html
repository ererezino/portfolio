A bit about me

I'm Zino Asamaige, a builder, photographer, and traveler based in Lagos, Nigeria. I spend my days working on Accrue and my evenings walking through the city with a camera.

Photography

Hobbyist photographer with a focus on travel and street photography. I document places, people, and the small moments in between. Mostly shooting on iPhone these days.

University of Bologna

MBA from the oldest university in the world, founded in 1088. An incredible experience living in Italy and learning alongside people from around the globe.

Racket Sports

Played tennis on and off for two years before discovering padel. Six months in and completely hooked. Always looking for a game if you're in Lagos.

Swimming

Walking

Long walks through cities are my meditation. There's something about moving through a place on foot that makes you see it completely differently. Also great for cardiovascular health.

Travel
