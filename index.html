I'm Samuel Ererezino Asamaige, a writer and photographer drawn to the quiet moments between the obvious ones.

My work explores the textures of everyday life: the way light falls on unfamiliar streets, the stories held in shared meals, the weight of places we've left and those we're yet to find.

Based between cities, always in transit.
