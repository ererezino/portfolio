About

I'm Samuel, a writer and photographer drawn to the quiet moments between the obvious ones. My work explores the textures of everyday life: the way light falls on unfamiliar streets, the stories held in shared meals, the weight of places we've left and those we're yet to find.

Through photography, I document travel, food, and street scenes. Through writing, I try to make sense of displacement, memory, and what it means to belong somewhere.

Based between cities, always in transit.
