About

I work at the intersection of product, operations, and compliance—building the systems that let companies move fast without breaking things.

Background

Currently COO at a fintech company operating across multiple jurisdictions, where I lead operations, compliance, and team development. Day to day, that means everything from FINTRAC registrations and FSCA licensing to building out equity compensation frameworks and managing international teams.

Before this, I spent time across product, growth, and operations roles. I like roles where the work is measurable and the outcomes are clear—where you can point to something and say "that's better now."

Outside of work, I write and photograph. I'm interested in place, memory, and belonging. You can find some of that work on this site.
